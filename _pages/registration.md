# Register for the conference

The “Radio and Newspapers” conference is free, but registration is required with the form below.

The conference takes place in person in Lausanne. For those unable to attend, it will be possible to follow the conference online, but not to interact with participants on site.

Registration for the in-person conference is open until **June 15**. Registration for online participation is open until midday on June 26.
